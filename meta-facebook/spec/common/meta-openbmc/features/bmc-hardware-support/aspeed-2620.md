The BMC firmware shall provide the necessary boot and driver support for the
Aspeed AST-2620 BMC.

The expected driver support includes the following:
- I2C Driver
- I3C Driver
- TPM Driver
- WDT Driver
- SPI Driver
- eSPI Driver
- USB 2.0 Driver
- PECI Driver
- Network/NCSI Driver
- GPIO Driver
- MTD Driver
- PCIe Driver
- PWM Driver
- TACH Driver
- EMMC Driver
- JTAG Driver/Support
- ADC Driver
- UART Driver
- SPGIO Driver
- Clock Driver
